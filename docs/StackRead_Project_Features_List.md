## **🧑‍💻 Reader (Subscriber)**

**Account & Subscription**

- Register via email, Google, Facebook, or institutional SSO
- Choose and manage subscription plan - basic, standard, or premium
- View subscription status, renewal date, and billing history
- Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel subscription
- Apply promo codes and referral discounts
- Multiple payment methods - card, mobile banking, PayPal
- Download and view invoices and receipts
- Device management - view and remove logged-in devices
- Profile - avatar, bio, reading preferences, and favourite genres
- Notification preferences - email, push, or in-app
- Privacy controls - hide reading history, make lists public/private
- Account deletion request করা

**Discovery & Search**

- Full-text search across entire catalogue
- Filter by format, genre, language, rating, availability, and publication year
- AI-powered personalised recommendations based on reading history
- Browse curated shelves, new arrivals, trending, and staff picks
- "Readers also enjoyed" and series continuation suggestions
- Saved searches and keyword alerts for new matching titles
- Discover titles by mood, theme, or reading time estimate

**Access & Reading**

- Unlimited access to subscription catalogue (within plan limits)
- Individual title purchase for titles outside subscription
- Borrow, renew, and return titles (for library-model titles)
- Place holds on limited-access titles with queue position visibility
- Automatic notification when a held title becomes available
- Offline download within active subscription or loan period
- Simultaneous access to eBook and audiobook of the same title

**Reading Experience**

- In-browser eBook and PDF reader
- Integrated audiobook player - playback speed, sleep timer, chapter skip
- Adjustable font size, typeface (including dyslexia-friendly), line spacing, margins, and theme (light/dark/system)
- Highlight text and add personal annotations
- In-book dictionary, Wikipedia lookup, and translation tool
- Text-to-speech for eBooks
- Chapter navigation and reading progress display
- Sync reading position across all devices in real time
- Continue reading from last position on any device

**Organisation & Lists**

- Create and name multiple reading lists (e.g. "Want to read", "Favourites", "Recommended to friends")
- Share reading lists publicly or with specific users
- Add personal tags to any title
- Export reading list as CSV or shareable link

**Reading Goals & Gamification**

- Set yearly or monthly reading targets
- Track reading streaks and daily reading time
- Earn badges for milestones - first borrow, 10 books completed, genre explorer, etc.
- View annual reading wrapped - total books, pages, hours, top genre, top author

**Community & Social**

- Write and submit reviews and star ratings
- Like and comment on other readers' reviews
- Follow other readers and authors and see their public activity
- Join or create book clubs
- Participate in book club discussion threads with shared annotations
- RSVP to author events, webinars, and platform programmes
- Share title or reading activity to social media

**Research Tools**

- Save citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, or Harvard format
- Build and export reference lists
- Annotated bibliography builder

**Help & Accessibility**

- Screen reader compatibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- High contrast and colour-blind modes
- Keyboard-only navigation
- Submit support tickets and live chat with help desk
- Onboarding tutorial and interactive help centre

## **✍️ Author / Publisher**

**Account & Organisation**

- Individual author account or multi-seat publisher account (up to defined seat limit)
- Role-based access within publisher account - owner, admin, editor, finance, uploader
- Author profile page - bio, photo, social links, website, genre tags, and full bibliography
- Verified author badge displayed on profile and all title pages
- Follow count and follower management
- Publisher brand page - logo, about, catalogue listing, and contact info

**Content Upload & Management**

- Upload eBooks (EPUB, PDF), audiobooks (MP3/M4B), and supplementary materials (reading guides, author notes)
- Bulk upload with metadata auto-fill via ISBN lookup
- Manual metadata editing - title, subtitle, series, genre, tags, synopsis, cover image, and keywords
- Cover image upload with built-in basic crop and resize tool
- Version management - update or replace files without losing access history
- Schedule titles for future release dates with countdown display
- Save titles as drafts before submission for review
- Unpublish or temporarily withdraw titles at any time
- Manage series groupings - series name, reading order, and instalment count
- Bundle related titles for promotional offers

**Rights & Licensing**

- Choose distribution model - subscription-only, purchase-only, or both
- Set simultaneous user access limits per title (1, 3, 5, or unlimited copies)
- Define loan durations for library-model titles (7, 14, 21, or 30 days)
- Set regional availability and geo-restrictions by country or region
- DRM settings - apply, modify, or waive digital rights management per title
- Choose licence model - one-copy-one-user, metered access, or perpetual access
- Set title expiry - auto-withdraw after a defined number of loans or a specific date
- Set promotional free-access windows with defined start and end dates

**Analytics & Reporting**

- Real-time dashboard - total reads, active readers, downloads, completions, and holds
- Reader engagement metrics - average read time, completion rate, chapter-by-chapter drop-off
- Geographic readership breakdown by country and city
- Search appearance and click-through rates (discoverability performance)
- Referral source breakdown - how readers found each title
- Performance comparison across titles and date ranges
- Subscription vs. purchase breakdown per title
- Downloadable reports in CSV or PDF format
- Scheduled automated report delivery to email

**Revenue & Royalties**

- Real-time royalty earnings dashboard - per title and total
- Royalty calculation transparency - rate applied, reads counted, payout formula
- Payout history and upcoming payment schedule
- Minimum payout threshold settings
- Multiple payout methods - bank transfer, PayPal, mobile banking
- Invoice generation and download
- Contract and agreement document storage
- Tax document management - VAT, withholding, W-8/W-9
- Revenue split visibility for co-authored or co-published titles
- Currency and regional payout settings

**Promotion & Marketing**

- Request featured placement or sponsored shelf listing (paid promotion)
- Create and submit promotional banners and excerpts for platform use
- New release announcements automatically pushed to followers
- Offer time-limited free access or discounted lending periods
- Promotional bundle creation with combined pricing
- Share embeddable title widgets for use on external websites or newsletters
- Affiliate link generation for external promotion tracking
- Pre-order listing setup with release date countdown

**Community & Engagement**

- Respond publicly to reader reviews
- Host virtual author Q&A sessions and live events through the portal
- Create and distribute book club resource kits - discussion guides, reading notes, author commentary
- Send announcements and newsletters to followers via integrated tool
- View and respond to reader questions submitted through the author profile page
- Participate in platform-organised author spotlights and themed collections

**Technical & Integration**

- ONIX metadata feed for catalogue syncing with external aggregators or retailers
- API access for automated title and metadata management
- Webhook support for real-time read, purchase, and payout event notifications
- Dedicated author/publisher support ticket queue with priority response
- Two-factor authentication for account security

## **🛠️ Admin / Staff**

**User Management**

- Create, edit, suspend, and delete reader, author, and publisher accounts
- Assign and modify roles and permission levels across all user types
- Bulk import users via CSV for institutional or partner onboarding
- View full user activity logs - logins, reads, searches, purchases, and downloads
- Manually reset passwords, verify emails, and unlock accounts
- Flag, warn, or ban accounts for policy or community guideline violations
- Manage subscription tiers and manually assign or override plan access
- Manage institutional access groups and partner account structures

**Catalogue Management**

- Add, edit, and remove any title in the catalogue
- Review queue - approve, reject, or request revision of author/publisher submitted titles
- Rejection reason templates and feedback messaging to authors
- Manage metadata for all titles - cover, synopsis, tags, categories, and keywords
- Create and manage curated shelves, featured collections, trending lists, and staff picks
- Set and override access limits and loan durations for any title
- Manage series, bundles, and cross-references between titles
- Run catalogue audits - flag missing metadata, broken files, or expired licences
- Batch metadata editing for multiple titles at once

**Circulation & Access**

- View all active reads, loans, holds, and waitlists across the system
- Manually adjust access or loan status on behalf of users
- Override access rules in exceptional cases
- Set system-wide access rules and default loan durations by format or collection
- Monitor and action overdue or expired access items

**Subscription & Billing Management**

- View and manage all active, paused, cancelled, and expired subscriptions
- Process manual subscription changes, upgrades, or refunds
- Configure subscription plans - pricing, features, trial periods, and limits
- Manage promo codes - create, set limits, expiry, and track usage
- View failed payment logs and trigger retry or notification workflows
- Manage partner and institutional subscription contracts
- Configure payment gateway settings - Stripe, PayPal, local payment providers

**Content Moderation**

- Review and approve or remove user-submitted reviews and ratings
- Moderate book club discussions and community threads
- Flag and investigate reported content or user conduct
- Issue warnings or take automated/manual action on policy-violating accounts
- Maintain and publish community guidelines and update history

**Author & Publisher Management**

- View and manage all author and publisher accounts and their catalogues
- Review and action payout requests
- Set and update royalty rates - by plan, format, region, or individual agreement
- Approve or reject promotional placement requests
- Manage verified author badge applications and approvals
- View author analytics across the platform for performance benchmarking

**Events & Programmes**

- Create, publish, and manage events - webinars, author talks, live Q&As, workshops
- Send event invitations and reminders to targeted user segments
- Track RSVPs, attendance, and post-event feedback
- Manage virtual event integrations - Zoom, Teams, or built-in live streaming
- Archive past events with recordings, transcripts, and resources

**Analytics & Reporting**

- Platform-wide dashboard - total users, active subscribers, revenue, catalogue size, and engagement
- Subscription analytics - MRR, ARR, churn rate, trial-to-paid conversion
- Circulation and access reports - most-read titles, formats, genres, and time periods
- Reader engagement reports - session length, retention, DAU/MAU
- Search analytics - top queries, zero-result searches, and filter usage
- Author and publisher performance overview across the platform
- Revenue and royalty payout summary reports
- Custom report builder with date range, user segment filters, and export to CSV or PDF
- Scheduled automated report delivery via email to stakeholders

**Financial & Royalty Admin**

- View and process all author and publisher royalty payouts
- Set and update platform commission rates and royalty formulas
- Approve invoices and trigger payment processing
- Manage payout schedules - monthly, quarterly, or on-demand
- Handle disputed earnings and payout corrections
- Generate financial summaries for auditing and tax purposes
- Manage VAT, GST, and local tax compliance settings

**System Configuration**

- Configure global platform settings - access rules, registration options, supported formats
- Manage authentication providers - SSO, LDAP, OAuth, and social login integrations
- Configure and customise email and push notification templates
- Set and update terms of service, privacy policy, refund policy, and cookie consent content
- Manage API keys, webhooks, and third-party integration credentials
- Configure DRM provider settings and enforcement rules
- Feature flag control - enable or disable features per user group, region, or subscription tier
- Localisation and multi-language settings
- CDN and media storage configuration for uploaded content

**Security & Compliance**

- Full audit logs for all admin actions - exportable and searchable
- Manage GDPR/PDPA data privacy requests - export or delete user data on request
- Monitor failed login attempts, brute force alerts, and suspicious activity
- Configure session timeout, password policy, and two-factor authentication enforcement
- Run and schedule security compliance reports
- Manage data retention policies and automated deletion schedules
- DMCA takedown request handling workflow
- Piracy and unauthorised sharing detection and reporting tools

**Support & Communication**

- Unified support ticket inbox across reader, author, and publisher queues
- Assign tickets to staff, set priority, and track resolution status and SLA
- Canned response templates for common issues
- Publish and update help centre articles, FAQs, and video tutorials
- Send targeted announcements - system maintenance, new features, policy updates - by user segment
- In-app notification broadcast tool
- Manage onboarding flows and tutorial content for each user role
- NPS and satisfaction survey tools - trigger, collect, and analyse responses
